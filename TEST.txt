From the s3k/projects/fs folder.

In the conf.h file three different use cases can be tested (parent cap, child cap, and independent cap).
Simply alter MEM_MODE to 1, 2, or 3 for the respective type.
The ENABLE_VIRTIO_QUEUE option blocks VirtIO driver from syncing with the device to illustrate a relevant queue entry during the testing process.
The PMP_CAP option toggles between memory capability and PMP capability.

As usual just run with "make qemu" assuming the disk-image has been made ("make disk-image").
